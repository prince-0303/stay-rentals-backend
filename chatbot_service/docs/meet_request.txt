A user can send a meet request to schedule a property visit. A user can request a maximum of 3 to 5 meets at a time.

Once a meet request is sent, the lister is notified and must confirm or reject the request within 1 hours. If the lister does not respond within 1 hours, the request expires automatically.

After the lister confirms, the visit is scheduled. The user must attend the visit at the agreed time.

If the user does not attend without prior notice, a warning is added to their account. More than 3 no-shows may result in a temporary account restriction of 24 hours to 3 days.

After visiting the property, the user can choose to proceed by paying the advance or simply decline without any charges.

On the property page, click the Request Meet button. The lister will receive a notification and can accept or decline.
