Advance Payment Policy

The advance payment amount is equivalent to 2 months rent of the property.

The advance is paid by the user to temporarily lock the property after a successful visit.

The advance amount is held directly by the lister, not the platform.

The lister receives the advance payment immediately once the transaction is completed.

Paying the advance temporarily locks the property for the user. If the user cancels after paying advance but before full confirmation, 50% of the advance is refunded.

Once the property is fully locked and confirmed, the advance becomes non-refundable.

If the lister cancels after receiving the advance, the user gets a 100% full refund within 3-5 business days.

The advance amount is completely refundable if the user completes the agreed rental term and provides a prior notice of at least one month before vacating the property.

The advance is collected after both the user and lister agree to proceed following the meet.
