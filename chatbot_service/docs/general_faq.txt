How do I search for a property?
Use the search bar on the homepage and enter your preferred location. You can filter results by number of rooms, beds, bathrooms, kitchen, and budget.

How do I schedule a visit?
Find a property you like and click the Request Meet button. The lister will be notified and will confirm within 1 hours.

Is there any fee to schedule a visit?
No. Scheduling a property visit is completely free.

How do I lock a property?
After visiting the property, if you decide to proceed, you can pay the advance amount from the property page to lock it.

What is the advance amount?
The advance is 2 months rent of the property you wish to lock.

Can I cancel after paying advance?
Yes, but only 50% of the advance will be refunded if you cancel before the property is fully locked.

What happens if the lister cancels?
You will receive a 100% refund within 3-5 business days.

How many visits can I schedule at once?
You can have a maximum of 3 to 5 active meet requests at a time.

What happens if I miss a scheduled visit?
A warning will be added to your account. Missing more than 3 visits may result in a temporary restriction of 24 hours to 3 days.

How do I contact support?
If you have any issues not covered here, please reach out to our support team through the platform.
