There is no fee collected for scheduling a property visit. Visits are completely free to book.

If a user does not attend a scheduled visit without informing in advance, a warning is issued to their account.
If a user misses visits more than 3 times, their account will be temporarily restricted for 24 hours to 3 days depending on the frequency of no-shows.

If a user pays an advance to temporarily lock a property and then cancels, 50% of the advance amount will be refunded.

If a property is completely locked and confirmed, the advance payment becomes non-refundable.

If the lister cancels a booking after receiving the advance payment, the user will receive a 100% full refund processed within 3-5 business days.

If a payment is deducted but the booking is not confirmed due to a technical error, the full amount will be refunded within 5-7 business days.

For any refund related queries, please contact our support team.

The refund of advance amount will be processed within 2–3 business days after the user has vacated the property and the property has been inspected, if applicable.
